Our Story

Rose City Coffee Co. isn't your average coffee shop. Although we work hard to give each of our customers delicious coffee and food, we most value the community we create within our business.

We aim to provide a comfortable space for everyone of any background or identity, regardless if they are coming to study, meet up with a friend, or simply to grab a coffee to go. That is why when you visit us, ou will be welcomed by our friendly baristas and get a choice of our many seating options. If you're lucky you can even sit in our popular booths with outlet access, an optimal place for getting some work done.

What ever part of your day it is, stop by and say hi! We know life can be hectic but sometimes it pays off to stop and smell the roses.
