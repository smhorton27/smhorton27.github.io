VECNA

Vecna, originally Henry Creel of Hawkins, was a human with psychokinetic abilities turned fearsome creature in the Upside Down. In 1979, after spending years being studied in Hawkins Lab, he tricked Eleven into helping him escape and murder all of the other test subjects. However, Eleven was able to fight him and banished him to the Upside Down. There he explore the possibilities of annihilating the human race with the help of the hive mind and began executing his plans in 1986 by opening up enough gates to completely destroy the world.
