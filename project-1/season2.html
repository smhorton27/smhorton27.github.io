DEMODOGS

"Demodog" refers to the adolescent stage in the Demogorgan's growth cycle. The size of a large dog, these species have the strength to take down a grown human and feast on their flesh. They also dug a tunnel system under Hawkins where they would take their victims to the Upside Down. Later it would be revealed that these creatures were fragments of the Mind Flayer. After defeating these creatures, they would be re-released two years later to act as weapons for the Soviet military.
