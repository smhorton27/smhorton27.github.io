THE DEMOGORGAN

The Demagorgan was a predatory humanoid creature that entered Hawkins, Indiana in November 1983 from the parallel dimension known as the Upside Down. Originally, it was released into the real world after Eleven made transdimensional contact with the creature, opening a gate between the dimensions. After the encounter, the monster was able to open several portals around Hawkins, and began hunting, killing, and kidnapping local townspeople including Will Byers and Barbara.
